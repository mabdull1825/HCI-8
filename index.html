Frequently Asked Questions

What is SecondStep?

SecondStep is a platform dedicated to supporting homeless individuals by connecting them with organizations and resources to help them take the next step towards stability.

Who can use this platform?

Anyone facing homelessness or those seeking support for housing, mental health, or employment can use SecondStep to find relevant resources in London.

How do I access the resources listed?

Each organization listed on our platform includes a link to their website where you can learn more about their services and how to get in touch.

Is this service free?

Yes, SecondStep is completely free to use. Our mission is to help individuals in need by providing accessible information about available support services.

Can I recommend an organization to be added?

Absolutely! Please contact us with the organization's details, and we will review and add it to our platform if it meets our criteria.

How often is the information updated?

We strive to keep the information up-to-date by regularly reviewing the listed resources and making changes as needed.
